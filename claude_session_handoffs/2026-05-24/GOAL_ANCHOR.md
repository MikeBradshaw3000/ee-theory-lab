## The central contribution: Regime II
A community can be highly *activated* — high activation density ρ, many founders,
events, capital — without becoming *coherent* — high Ψ, self-reinforcing at the system
level. ρ and Ψ are governed by distinct dynamics in the two-stage cascade
((v, c, r) → Λ → ρ → Ψ), separated by the sign-change in μ(ρ). μ(ρ) must stay
non-positive in the low-density limit because coherence is relational — mutual
reinforcement requires a multiplicity. The counter-intuitive, testable claim the field
does not already have: **more activity does not equal ecosystem health.**
