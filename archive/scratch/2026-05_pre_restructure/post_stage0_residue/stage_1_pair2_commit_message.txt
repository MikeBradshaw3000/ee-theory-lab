  formal-primitive-substitution terms. Section 2 specifies permitted

  the Open Element labeling discipline.

  Twelve sections covering theory-level documents, Phase 4B
  specifications, Tier 3 canonical implementation, reg_01 cluster,
  substrate data and Tier 2 outputs, diagnostics, operations logs and
  routing artifacts, Stage 0 inventory, foundational document set,
  instantiation kit, naming-collision citation discipline, workspace
  and tools. Authoritative for where artifacts live and what authority
  they carry; not authoritative for artifact content (each artifact is
  its own authority).
